Retail

The competitive landscape of retail sees it operating on thin margins and ever changing trends across many product lines. By implementing sustainable practices across the business presents enormous opportunities to maintain margins and profitability.

Improving your bottom line and environmental performance.

Retailers are in a unique front line position with consumers to advance sustainability by educating consumers and offering "sustainable" products. At the same time retailers are also required to respond to market trends and the consumer demands for greater supply chain transparency.

The opportunity for retailers to influence suppliers to develop products that are more sustainable is considerable given their commercial imperative to stock products that sell. Empauer works alongside leading retailers to develop and implement solutions that address today's sustainability challenges and tomorrow's opportunities.

Our global network of experts will work with you to understand the material issues with the greatest potential for business, environmental, and societal outcomes across the retail chain.
